# Refund Policy

## Service Provider Information
geministorybook.life is operated by TAO NIE, an independent developer based in China.

## Refund Terms

1. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all our paid storytelling services.
2. Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the purchase date.
3. To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@geministorybook.life.

## Refund Process

1. Submit your refund request with your order details.
2. Our team will review your request within 2 business days.
3. If approved, the refund will be processed through your original payment method.
4. Refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear in your account.

## Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be denied if:
1. The 14-day period has expired
2. The storytelling service has been substantially used
3. Terms of service have been violated
4. The purchase was made fraudulently
