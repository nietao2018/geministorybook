## Refund Policy

1. We offer a limited refund policy that applies only to specific circumstances.
2. You may request a refund within 7 days of purchase if our service experiences significant technical failures that prevent you from using paid features.
3. Refund requests must be submitted via email to Support@Converters.pro with detailed information about the issues encountered.
4. We reserve the right to review all refund requests and make decisions on a case-by-case basis.
5. Refunds are generally not provided for services already used or credits already consumed.
6. If a refund is approved, funds will be returned to the original payment method, and processing may take 5-10 business days.
7. We do not accept refund requests based on change of mind or dissatisfaction with generated results.
